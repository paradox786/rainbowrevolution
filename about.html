About Us

This is our group project of Design Thinking, a short term course held in our winter break.Hoping to make it bigger by bringing it in real world.

We are three undergrad students from IIT Delhi.After interviewing a bunch of people of Delhi, we identified that the major problem is the mind-set of people.They are taking environment as for guaranteed.They know that one should be educated and healthy for leading a good life but they ignore the environmental issues and don't care to bother about them.So, we have taken intiative to inculcate the importance of environment in people's mind.Make them aware how it is going to affect in their daily life and how they can help us to make this world a better place !
